About me,

Hi, I’m Gaurav Chanda, a passionate Computer Science and Engineering student at Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology. I specialize in frontend development and have a solid grasp of modern technologies, including JavaScript, React, Node.js, and the MERN stack.

One of my most significant projects is BrowserDotCom, a custom web browser built using JavaFX. It features multi-tab browsing, bookmark management, keyboard shortcuts, and an integrated browsing history powered by JDBC. This project challenged me to combine usability and performance, deepening my knowledge of software design and user experience.

My toolkit extends to HTML, CSS, Python, MySQL, and Git, enabling me to tackle diverse challenges and create innovative solutions. Beyond coding, I’m an avid problem-solver who enjoys competitive programming and staying updated on emerging tech trends.

Feel free to connect with me—I’m always excited to collaborate and explore new opportunities!
